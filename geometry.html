\(d(A,B) = \sqrt{(x_B-x_A)^2 + (y_B-y_A)^2 + (z_B-z_A)^2}\)
Điểm - Mặt phẳng
\(d(M,(P)) = \frac{|ax_0+by_0+cz_0+d|}{\sqrt{a^2+b^2+c^2}}\)
Điểm - Đường thẳng
\(d(M,\Delta) = \frac{|[\vec{AM}, \vec{u}]|}{|\vec{u}|}\)

\(d(\Delta_1,\Delta_2) = \frac{|[\vec{u_1},\vec{u_2}] \cdot \vec{A_1A_2}|}{\|[\vec{u_1},\vec{u_2}]\|}\)
Đường thẳng // Mặt phẳng

🟢 GÓC ▼
Giữa 2 vector
\(\cos\varphi = \frac{|\vec{u}\cdot\vec{v}|}{\|\vec{u}\|\|\vec{v}\|}\)
Giữa 2 đường thẳng
\(\cos\varphi = \frac{|\vec{u_1}\cdot\vec{u_2}|}{\|\vec{u_1}\|\|\vec{u_2}\|}\)
Đường thẳng & Mặt phẳng
\(\sin\varphi = \frac{|\vec{u}\cdot\vec{n}|}{\|\vec{u}\|\|\vec{n}\|}\)
Giữa 2 mặt phẳng
\(\cos\varphi = \frac{|\vec{n_1}\cdot\vec{n_2}|}{\|\vec{n_1}\|\|\vec{n_2}\|}\)
